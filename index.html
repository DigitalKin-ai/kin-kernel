Welcome to kin-kernel’s documentation!

kin-kernel is a Python package developed by DigitalKin.ai that offers a framework for building and deploying autonomous agents, known as Cells, within the Internet of Agents (IoA). This platform democratizes access to artificial intelligence, making it readily available to innovators, enthusiasts, and visionaries alike.

Introduction

kin-kernel is designed to provide developers, with a powerful and user-friendly platform for collaboration and creation within the realm of AI. By adhering to DigitalKin’s standards, kin-kernel facilitates the development of Cells that can be seamlessly published and integrated into DigitalKin’s IoA, ensuring a standardized approach to AI agent development.

With kin-kernel, users can leverage the transformative power of AI to enhance productivity and gain foresight into the future. It embodies DigitalKin’s mission to make complex AI technologies easily accessible and to place AI at the service of humanity, contributing to the collective effort in shaping the digital transformation.

Source Code

The source code for kin-kernel is available on GitHub.

Requirements

kin-kernel requires Python version 3.10.11 or higher.
